10. while Loops

Repeat a block until a condition becomes false.

Print numbers 1-5 with while loop.

Understand indefinite iteration.

	1. Repeat a block until a condition becomes false - Completed
	
	i = 1
	while i < 6: # i is less than 6
		print(i)
		i += 1
	
	2. Print numbers 1-5 with while loop - Completed
	
	i = 1
	while i < 6: # i is less than 6
		print(i)
		i += 1
	
	3. Understand indefinite iteration - Completed

	i = 1
	while True:
		print(i)
